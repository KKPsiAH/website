Alpha Eta | Gator Band 5K

The Second Annual Gator Band 5K

Join us for the Gator Band 5K, an annual event put on by the brothers of Kappa Kappa Psi in order to raise money to benefit the Gator Band and the College of the Arts! Proceeds from this event will go back to accomplishing our own service projects as well as fostering musicianship in the community of Gainesville.

In-Person 5K

The in-person race will take place Saturday, March 19 at 8 a.m. Our start and finish line is the Gator Band Practice Field, located at 1273 Gale Lemerand Drive. After you cross the finish, participants can celebrate by enjoying refreshments and a photo-op!

Virtual 5K

If you're unable to join us in-person but still want to support the Gator Band, you can register to run virtually! After registering, run the 5K at a time and date of your choice. Tag us at @kkpsiah along your route so we can share it on social media and send you your shirt!

Registration

Online registration is $25 for community members and $20 for students with a valid UF ID. Prices increase to $30 and $25 on the day of the event, so register now to save!
