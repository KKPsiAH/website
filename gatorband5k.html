The 2nd Annual Gator Band 5K

Join us for The Gator Band 5K, an annual event put on by the brothers of Kappa Kappa Psi in order to raise money to benefit the Gator Band and the College of the Arts, other money that is raised during the time of this event will go back to accomplishing our own service projects as well as fostering musicianship in the community of Gainesville.

The 5K will take place on Saturday March 19th, with a race start time of 8:00am, this event will start and finish at the Gator band practice field, or better known as Pony Field at 1273 Gale Lemerand Dr, Gainesville, FL 32608.

Following the event there will be a BBQ held for Alumni of the Gator band, and the Alpha Eta Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi at Gator Band Field.

Online Registration is $25 for community members and $20 for students (with valid UF ID), day-of registration will be $30 for community members and $25 for students so register now to save!
