Were you involved with leadership in your high school band? Are you passionate about music? Do you want to give back to a program you love?

Then you may want to consider coming out to our recruitment events! As a national honorary service organization, we provide our time and service to the college and university band programs. The University of Florida's band program gives students the opportunity to continue their love of music; however, this opportunity would not exist without the help of both Kappa Kappa Psi and our sister organization Tau Beta Sigma. Brothers of Kappa Kappa Psi are responsible for setting up the practice field and concert stage, filling coolers for games, handing out water after pregame and Gatorade after halftime and organizing the uniform room. In addition, we also complete various service projects.

FAQs

Kappa Kappa Psi is a fraternity that allows all gender identities. While there are other chapters that happen to be only male, our chapter here at the University of Florida is proud to have members of all genders.
Not at all! We are a band fraternity, and our only requirement is that you are a member of a University of Florida band ensemble. Our Brothers have majored in all sorts of fields across all colleges of the university.
As students at the University of Florida, we know that time commitments are a big deal. We like to say that membership is equivalent to a 2-3 credit course. You can learn more about our work and time commitments at recruitment events!
Upon initiation, new Brothers pay a one-time initiation fee of around $130 that goes to National Headquarters to register you and pay national dues for the academic year. This amount is subject to change. Active members pay national dues annually and chapter dues each semester, the amount of which are determined at the beginning of each semester. These payments are always relatively low and are nothing compared to other fraternities. Our chapter's treasurer can give you a more accurate estimate and also help you with payment plans.
We take up many projects revolving around our bands and music programs at the University of Florida. These projects range anywhere from providing water and Gatorade to the Gator Marching Band for practices and football games, to helping transport a Grand Piano that we donated across town to a school in need of one. We are always open to new ideas for projects!
Unlike most traditional fraternities, Kappa Kappa Psi does not have a house to host events, and all of our official events are dry. We have many social events throughout the year, but alcohol is not permitted at any event sponsored or hosted by the Alpha Eta chapter. To protect the integrity of our fraternity, we do not permit brothers who are of the proper drinking age to consume alcohol while wearing regalia or property of Kappa Kappa Psi.
